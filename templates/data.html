Titanic Dataset and Features
Our team accessed this dataset through OpenML.com.
It includes all the retrieved data from the Titanic, most importantly, who survived.

The dataset features are explained below:

pclassTicket Class (i.e. 1st class, 2nd class, 3rd class)
survivedSurvived or not
nameName of passenger
sexMale or female
ageAge of passenger
sibsp# of siblings of passenger
parch# of parents/children on board
ticketTicket Number
fareCost of ticket
cabinCabin number
embarkedLocation boarded from (C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton)
boatWhich boat the survivors made it out on
bodyWeight of passenger
home.destDestination

In total, the dataset has 1309 people and 14 features collected for each.
