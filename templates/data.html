The DataSet
Our team recieved this data set from Kaggle.com.
The dataset includes all the retrieved data from the Titanic, and most importantly, who survived.
The dataset features are explained below:

- pclass: Ticket Class (i.e. 1st class, 2nd class, 3rd class)
- survivied: Survived or not
- name: Name of passenger
- sex: Male or female
- age: Age of passenger
- sibsp: # of siblings of passenger
- parch: # of parents/children on board
- ticket: Ticket Number
- fare: Cost of ticket
- cabin: Cabin number
- embarked: Location boarded on (C = Cherbourg, Q = Queenstown, S = Southampton)
- boat: Which boat the survivors made it out on
- body: Weight of passenger (However, somewhat unclear)
- home.dest: Destination

In total, the data set has 1309 people and 14 features collected for each.
